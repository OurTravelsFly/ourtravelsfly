Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I book a flight?
You can book flights directly through our website using the booking widget on the homepage. Simply enter your departure and destination cities, select dates, and choose your preferred class.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies by airline and hotel. Most bookings can be cancelled up to 24 hours before departure with minimal fees. Check your booking confirmation for specific details.
How can I get a refund?
Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation approval. Please contact our support team with your booking reference number to initiate a refund request.
Do you offer travel insurance?
Yes! We offer comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost luggage, and more. You can add insurance during the booking process.
How do I change my booking?
You can modify your booking through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team. Changes may be subject to fees depending on the airline/hotel policy.
